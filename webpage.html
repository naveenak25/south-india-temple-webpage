Mahabalipuram temple

  • It is a structural temple, built with blocks of granite, dating from the 8th century AD
  • The site was a busy port during the reign of Narasimhavarman II of the Indian Pallava dynasty.
  • It has been classified as a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1984.
  • It is one of the oldest structural (versus rock-cut)
  • It is one of the stone temples of Southern India.

Brihadishwara temple

  • Hindu temple built in a Chola architectural style located on the south bank of the Cauvery river in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • It included gopura, the main temple, its massive tower, inscriptions, frescoes, and sculptures predominantly related to Shaivism, but also of Vaishnavism and Shaktism.
  • The temple is one of the most visited tourist attractions in Tamil Nadu.

Meenakshi temple

  • Arulmigu Meenakshi Sundaraswarar Temple is a historic Hindu temple located on the southern bank of the Vaigai River in the temple city of Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • The west tower(gopuram)of the temple is the model based on which the Tamil Nadu State Emblem is designed.
  • The temple is one of the most visited tourist attractions in Tamil Nadu.
